What Happens When You're Not On-Site?

Distance is the biggest risk in construction. Without an independent eye, minor oversights become major financial liabilities.

Subpar Materials

Contractors may swap specified premium materials for cheaper alternatives when no one is checking the receipts and intake.

Design Deviations

Changes made on-site without approval that affect the aesthetics, structural integrity, or value of your property.

Hidden Defects

Waterproofing failures, electrical grounding issues, and hollow tiles—problems that only appear after you've paid the final installment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an inspection cost?

Pricing depends on the property type (Apartment vs. Villa) and square footage. Typically, it starts from $200 USD for a standard apartment snagging. Contact us for a precise quote.

Can you fix the defects for me?

We are strictly an inspection and oversight firm. This maintains our neutrality. However, we oversee the repairs done by your contractor to ensure they are done correctly.

Do you travel outside of Da Nang?

Yes, we cover the greater Central Vietnam area including Hue, Lang Co, Da Nang, and Hoi An.
